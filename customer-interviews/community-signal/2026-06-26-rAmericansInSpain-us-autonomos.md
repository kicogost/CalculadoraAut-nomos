## The two findings that matter

1. **The desired outcome, stated perfectly:** *"I never think about my taxes."* That's the
   emotional north star — **set-and-forget peace of mind**, not features. Whatever the
   solution (human or app), the JOB is "make me never have to worry about errors or
   deadlines." Our product must deliver *that feeling*.
2. **Trust transfers by recommendation.** The instant someone said "I have an amazing tax
   attorney," **4+ people asked for the contact.** People adopt a *recommended* human over
   an unknown app. → referrals/word-of-mouth are how trust spreads here — central to GTM,
   and the barrier a new app must overcome.

Plus a concrete, ownable pain: **missing payments → fines + losing the tarifa-plana
discount** → deadline/payment tracking (our reminders) is genuinely valued.

## What it means for Provisio

- **Lead on the emotional promise:** *"no vuelvas a pensar en tus impuestos"* / peace of
  mind — delivered for the ICP via automation + correctness (not via a human).
- **Build referrals into GTM from day one** — trust spreads person-to-person; a referral
  loop is both acquisition and the trust-transfer mechanism.
- **Expats are a distinct segment** wanting a *trusted, English-speaking, done-for-you*
  solution (Renn / YourMatePat play). Our self-serve simplicity is the play for the
  **Spanish, software-native** ICP — don't let expat "get a gestor" pessimism reshape the
  core product.
- **Deadlines/penalties** (tarifa-plana loss) → keep the proactive reminders sharp.
