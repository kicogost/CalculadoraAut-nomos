## What it means for Provisio

**Validates**
- The **real-time "cuánto apartar / cuánto va a Hacienda"** hook — explicitly called
  *imprescindible*; blind quarterly delegation called *irresponsable*. This is the
  ICP voice we want.
- **Privacy/trust** matters more than expected — distrust is the climate (they suspect
  even a peer asker is Hacienda). "Tus datos son tuyos, nunca se comparten" lands hard.
- **Deductions clarity = take-home** → counters the "me roban" feeling by helping people
  *legally* pay less. Strong, ownable wedge.

**Challenges**
- The **trust barrier** is real even in the ICP (→ now a standing interview probe).
- **Crowded + AI substitutes** (Quantum, AphexIce, ChatGPT) → differentiation
  (autónomo-only, dead-simple, the set-aside hook, privacy) is what wins, not features.
- **Price-sensitive** (45€ union option) → our annual pricing must read as obviously
  cheaper than a monthly gestor (it is: 89/199€/yr vs 540–840€/yr).
- We **cannot serve the "B" economy** (illegal) — but it signals the legal burden feels
  unbearable; our job is to make the *legal* path less painful + ensure no overpaying.
